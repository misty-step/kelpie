For operators running a herd of coding agents in terminals.

Triage your coding-agent fleet from your phone.

kelpie puts every omp agent in your herdr workspaces on one screen: who is blocked on you, who is working, who is done. Answer asks, steer sessions, drop to the raw terminal — from the couch, over your tailnet.

MIT-licensed. One Rust binary, a zero-build PWA, no cloud, no accounts.

Get started View on GitHub

What it does

inbox Attention-first sort: pending asks float up, working hums, idle recedes. Recency breaks ties. asks When an agent blocks on a question, the options surface as buttons. Answer in one tap.
sessions Full transcript — markdown, tool calls, thinking — plus a real composer with model and reasoning-effort pickers. terminal When chat is not enough: the raw PTY screen with Enter, Esc, Ctrl+C, arrows, and Tab.
private Binds 127.0.0.1 and rides your tailnet. Nothing leaves your machines. zero config Workspaces get a deterministic icon and hue hashed from their name. Fleets churn; kelpie keeps up.

Quickstart

git clone https://github.com/misty-step/kelpie && cd kelpie
cargo run --release                  # bridge + PWA on 127.0.0.1:8787
tailscale serve --bg localhost:8787  # reach it from your phone

Open it on your phone and Add to Home Screen. Requires herdr running locally and omp agents in its panes. Full setup in the README.
